About Ivi Bookstore

As the world saw a surging popularity of digital shopping driven by its convenience, I have created a website for book enthusiasts that supplies the demand for purchasing books online.

Through the creation of this website, I have sought to create a haven where bibliophiles can traverse the realms of fiction, delve into the depths of non-fiction, and explore the uncharted territories of poetry, all with the ease that only digital shopping can afford. Every feature, every design element, and every piece of content has been meticulously crafted to not only facilitate the act of purchasing books but to elevate it into a holistic and enriching experience.


Purpose

Introducing Ivi, a bookstore that allows the customers to satisfy their needs of buying online, with its meticulously curated books collection thoughtfully categorised for ease of use.

Adapting to the trend of doorstep delivery, Ivi bookstore maximises the customer experience by erasing the difficulties in visiting physical stores. Prioritising the user-centric designs, the website's UI creates an appealing and easy-to-use visual to ensure satisfaction through simple interactions with the webpage. Every click allows clients to make a step closer to the emerging world with the assistance of technology, where every purchasing experience is valued and unforgettable.
